## H1

Logistics Management Platforms And Shipment Visibility

## Summary Copy

Logistics businesses depend on coordination, visibility, documentation, and timely customer communication. When shipment data is spread across spreadsheets, email, phone calls, and disconnected systems, both customers and internal teams lose time.

Logic Unit's Seagold case study shows how a logistics management and shipment tracking platform can centralize shipment data, document management, notifications, customer self-service, and operational dashboards.
