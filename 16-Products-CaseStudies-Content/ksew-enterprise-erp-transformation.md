KS&EW Enterprise ERP Transformation

## Summary

Karachi Shipyard & Engineering Works launched a major digital transformation initiative to modernize engineering, procurement, project management, manufacturing, and finance operations.

Logic Unit participated in the implementation of an enterprise-wide ERP platform designed to unify operations and improve visibility across complex shipbuilding and engineering projects.

CTA: `Discuss Enterprise Collaboration`

## Challenge

KS&EW operated multiple functions across shipbuilding, engineering, procurement, maintenance, construction, and finance. Disconnected systems limited visibility and operational efficiency.

## Solution

The ERP ecosystem covered project management, procurement management, engineering integration, contract management, and financial management.

## Key Capabilities

- Budgeting, scheduling, forecasting, and risk management
- RFQs, purchasing, supplier management, and material tracking
- CAD integration, design workflows, and document management
- Contract variations, retentions, payments, and certificates
- Project accounting, revenue recognition, and financial reporting

## Technical Highlights

IFS Applications ERP, enterprise integrations, engineering data integration, reporting dashboards, workflow automation, and project controls.

## Results

The transformation supported unified processes, improved reporting accuracy, stronger project controls, greater procurement visibility, reduced information silos, and scalable enterprise architecture.
