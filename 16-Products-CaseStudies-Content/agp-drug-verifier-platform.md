## H1

AGP Drug Verifier Platform

## Summary

AGP partnered with Logic Unit to develop a drug authentication ecosystem designed to combat counterfeit medicines and support pharmaceutical compliance.

The platform enables consumers to verify medicine authenticity through barcode scanning while giving AGP visibility into supply chain activity and scan analytics.

CTA: `Discuss A Healthcare Verification Platform`

## Challenge

AGP needed to address counterfeit medicine risk, regulatory compliance, product traceability, consumer trust, and supply chain visibility.

## Solution

Logic Unit developed a verification ecosystem with unique product serialization, 2D barcode generation, consumer verification, manufacturer dashboards, analytics, and compliance reporting.

## Key Features

- Instant medicine verification
- Product authentication
- 2D barcode generation
- Scan tracking
- Geographic analytics
- Counterfeit detection
- Regulatory compliance support

## Results

The platform improved consumer confidence, supply chain visibility, counterfeit monitoring, regulatory readiness, and brand protection.
