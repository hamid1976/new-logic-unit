## Hero Copy

The Multi-Function Console, or MFCC, is a mission-critical command and control workstation designed for maritime, defense, and surveillance environments.

Developed by Logic Unit for demanding operational conditions, MFCC combines ergonomic design, ruggedized hardware, and advanced system integration capabilities into a single operator platform.

## H2: Operational Challenge

Operators in defense and maritime environments require continuous monitoring, rapid decision-making tools, long-duration comfort, and integration with multiple mission systems.

Standard commercial workstations are often unsuitable for these operating conditions.

## H2: Key Features

### Dual 24-Inch Marine Displays

Support simultaneous monitoring of radar, sonar, navigation, and video feeds.

### Touch Input Display

Provide dedicated operational controls and mission interaction.

### Ergonomic Design

Designed to reduce operator fatigue, physical strain, and performance degradation during long operating periods.

### Ruggedized Construction

Built for marine environments, vibration resistance, humidity resistance, and continuous operation.

### Mission Flexibility

Suitable for maritime operations, surveillance systems, command centers, and defense applications.

## H2: Integration Capabilities

MFCC supports integration with C4I systems, navigation platforms, video systems, sensor networks, and mission management systems.
