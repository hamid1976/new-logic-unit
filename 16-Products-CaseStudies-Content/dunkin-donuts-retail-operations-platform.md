## H1

Dunkin' Donuts Pakistan Retail Operations & Sales Management Platform

## Summary

Dunkin' Donuts Pakistan needed real-time visibility into sales, operations, inventory, and performance across multiple retail outlets.

Logic Unit developed a mobile-first operational platform that digitized reporting, standardized workflows, and improved communication between branches and management.

CTA: `Discuss A Retail Operations Platform`

## Challenge

As the organization expanded, management faced delayed sales reporting, manual data collection, inconsistent branch processes, limited operational visibility, and communication inefficiencies.

## Solution

Logic Unit developed a hybrid mobile application for branch teams and management to collaborate through a centralized digital platform.

## Key Features

- Daily sales reporting
- Branch performance monitoring
- Operations dashboard
- Task management
- Internal communication
- Analytics and KPI reporting

## Results

The platform supported faster reporting cycles, improved operational visibility, better communication, increased accountability, and data-driven decision-making.

## Technology Stack

Ionic, Angular, mobile APIs, cloud infrastructure, and secure authentication.
