## H1

Smart Salik: Digital Toll Management Platform For Dubai RTA

## Summary

Dubai's Roads and Transport Authority needed a mobile platform to improve how motorists interact with the Salik electronic toll system.

Logic Unit contributed to the Smart Salik mobile application, giving users real-time access to account balances, vehicle management, trip history, recharge services, and violation tracking.

CTA: `Discuss A Government Digital Platform`

## Challenge

Users had limited account visibility, inconvenient balance checks, manual recharge processes, poor access to trip history, difficulty managing violations, and reliance on customer service channels.

## Solution

The mobile application centralized Salik account, vehicle, payment, toll activity, and notification workflows into one self-service experience.

## Key Features

- Account management
- Instant recharge
- Vehicle management
- Trip history
- Violation management
- Low-balance and payment notifications

## Technical Highlights

iOS and Android applications, government system integrations, secure payment gateway, real-time synchronization, OCR capabilities, and push notification services.

## Results

The application achieved 1M+ downloads, increased digital adoption, reduced customer service dependency, improved user satisfaction, and strengthened self-service capabilities.
