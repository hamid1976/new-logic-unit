## Hero Copy

HULM POS is a comprehensive business management platform developed by Logic Unit for retailers, restaurants, franchises, and multi-location businesses that need one centralized system for daily operations.

Unlike traditional POS systems that focus only on billing, HULM POS supports sales, inventory, procurement, CRM, employee management, reporting, and regulatory compliance through a connected operating platform.

## H2: Built For Growing Retail And Service Businesses

Many businesses still manage billing, inventory, procurement, customer records, reporting, and accounting through disconnected tools. This creates inventory discrepancies, manual reporting, poor customer visibility, delayed decisions, compliance challenges, and revenue leakage.

HULM POS helps reduce those gaps by giving teams real-time visibility across stock, sales, customers, vendors, and business performance.

## H2: Core Modules

### Point Of Sale

Fast billing workflows with barcode scanning, multiple payment methods, discounts, promotions, refund management, and receipt generation.

### Inventory Management

Real-time product tracking, multi-location inventory, batch management, stock transfers, and low-stock alerts.

### Purchase And Vendor Management

Create purchase orders, manage vendors, track supplier performance, and monitor procurement activity.

### Customer Relationship Management

Customer profiles, purchase history, loyalty programs, customer segmentation, and marketing campaign support.

### Employee Management

Track employee activity, shift performance, sales performance, and access permissions.

### Reporting And Analytics

Dashboards for sales trends, inventory insights, profitability analysis, and business KPIs.

### FBR Integration

Support for tax-compliant invoicing, FBR integration, and digital record keeping.

## H2: Industries Served

HULM POS supports retail stores, restaurants, bakeries, pharmacies, supermarkets, fashion retailers, electronics stores, and franchise networks.

## H2: Deployment And Integrations

Deployment options include cloud-based, on-premise, and hybrid deployment. Integration areas include FBR systems, payment gateways, ERP systems, accounting platforms, eCommerce platforms, and mobile applications.

## H2: Why HULM POS

HULM POS is more than a point of sale system. It is a complete business operating platform designed to support growth, visibility, inventory accuracy, customer retention, and operational excellence.
