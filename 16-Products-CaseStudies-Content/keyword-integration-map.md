## Keyword Placement Rules

- Homepage should use broad company keywords, not every product keyword.
- Ecosystem index should use `product ecosystem`, `SaaS product ecosystem`, `industry-specific SaaS platforms`, and link to all product pages.
- Product pages should combine the product keyword with the broader Logic Unit positioning.
- Case study pages should use proof keywords such as `product engineering company`, `software platforms for real industries`, `digital transformation`, and the specific project category.
- Industry pages should link to matching products and case studies:
  - Retail: HULM POS, Dunkin' Donuts Pakistan, GiftWifts
  - Healthcare: Hospitello, Novo Nordisk, AGP
  - Logistics: Seagold, Smart Salik, mPay
  - Manufacturing: Titan CMMS, KS&EW
  - Facilities: Titan CMMS, MFCC
  - Livestock: AnimalCare360
