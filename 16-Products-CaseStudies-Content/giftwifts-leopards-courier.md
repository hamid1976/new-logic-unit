## Summary

GiftWifts positioned Leopards as more than a logistics company by creating a new digital commerce channel for gifting, delivery tracking, and corporate ordering.

## Challenge

The opportunity was to build a digital gifting experience that combined product discovery, personalization, checkout, and delivery visibility with Leopards' logistics operations.

## Solution

The platform integrated commerce, personalization, corporate gifting, payment processing, and delivery tracking into one customer-facing digital experience.

## Key Features

- Product catalog management
- Category management
- Dynamic promotions
- Gift messages and packaging options
- Corporate gifting portal
- Secure checkout
- Real-time delivery tracking

## Business Impact

GiftWifts opened a digital commerce channel for Leopards while improving convenience for retail and corporate gifting customers.
