# CEO Strategy Brief

## CEO View

Logic Unit should not be marketed like a software agency.

The website should position Logic Unit as a product engineering and SaaS operating company that builds and operates industry-specific software platforms for real industries.

The current website has the right direction, but it needs:

- clearer business model explanation
- stronger proof
- stronger page architecture
- audience-specific CTAs
- better industry depth
- better partnership depth
- indexing and canonical fixes
- content that supports SEO and conversion together

## Main Positioning Statement

Logic Unit builds and operates industry-specific SaaS platforms for real industries.

## Supporting Positioning

- Product engineering company
- SaaS operating company
- Industry-specific SaaS platform builder
- Product ecosystem operator
- Technology backbone for real industry software
- Strategic partner for industry SaaS growth

## What Logic Unit Provides

Based on the current website, Logic Unit provides:

- industry-specific SaaS platforms
- product engineering
- SaaS architecture
- multi-tenant systems
- data infrastructure
- AI and IoT integration
- product ecosystem operation
- strategic partnerships
- system integrator relationships
- enterprise collaboration

## What Logic Unit Should Not Claim Without New Source

- software agency services
- custom software development services
- web development services
- mobile app development services
- ERP implementation
- CRM implementation
- IT outsourcing
- managed IT services
- case studies
- customer logos
- awards
- certifications
- revenue or traction claims

## Website Audience Priorities

| Audience | What They Need From The Website | Primary CTA |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategic partners | Understand ecosystem model and collaboration routes | Partner With Us |
| System integrators | Understand implementation/integration relationship potential | Start System Integrator Conversation |
| Enterprises | Understand platform collaboration and digital infrastructure relevance | Contact Logic Unit |
| Investors / expansion partners | Understand long-term product ecosystem model | Corporate Inquiry |
| Talent | Understand company direction and product ecosystem | Talent Conversation |

## Brand Voice

The website should sound:

- precise
- executive
- technical but readable
- operational
- long-term
- credible

Avoid:

- hype
- generic agency language
- unsupported claims
- vague transformation slogans
- keyword stuffing
