Hulm Solutions: Cloud POS & Business Management Software Built for Pakistan and the World
When we started Logic Unit over 15 years ago, we saw the same problem repeating itself across every industry we worked with. A shop owner in Karachi was writing sales receipts by hand. A cafe manager in Lahore had no idea how much stock was left in the kitchen. A clothing retailer in Islamabad was running three separate Excel sheets to track customers, purchases, and sales — and still making errors at month-end. The problem was not a lack of effort. The problem was a lack of the right tools.
That realisation drove us to build Hulm Solutions — a suite of cloud-based SaaS products designed from the ground up for the way Pakistani businesses actually operate, while being robust enough for global deployment.
Hulm Solutions is not just software. It is the operating system for your business — connecting your sales floor, stockroom, purchasing team, vendors, and analytics into one unified, cloud-based platform.

The Problem Pakistani Businesses Were Facing — and Still Face Today
Over a decade and a half of working with retail stores, restaurants, pharmacies, salons, bakeries, and manufacturing units, we identified the core pain points that cost businesses thousands of rupees every single month:
Sales transaction management done manually led to billing errors, undercharging, and disputes with customers.
Inventory tracking was either non-existent or delayed — owners discovered stockouts only after losing a sale.
Tax reporting and FBR compliance was a quarterly headache, with no clean audit trail to reference.
Purchase orders were tracked on paper or in scattered spreadsheets, causing vendor payment disputes.
Customer data lived in the owner's memory — no purchase history, no loyalty tracking, no receivables visibility.
Logistic operations had zero real-time visibility — deliveries were managed through phone calls and handwritten logs.
Reporting was either absent or too late — business owners made decisions based on last month's data, not today's.

These are not minor inconveniences. They are the difference between a profitable business and one that bleeds slowly until it collapses. And they are exactly the challenges Hulm Solutions was engineered to solve.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hulm Solutions
What types of businesses can use Hulm Solutions?
Hulm Solutions is designed for retail stores, restaurants, cafes, pharmacies, salons, bakeries, clothing stores, electric shops, and manufacturing units. It is industry-configurable, meaning the platform adapts its features and layout to match how each business type actually operates.
Is Hulm Solutions FBR compliant?
Yes. All invoicing and billing modules within Hulm Solutions are built to meet FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) requirements, including compliant receipt printing, sales tax tracking, and reporting formats suitable for tax filing in Pakistan.
Can I manage multiple branches with Hulm Solutions?
Absolutely. Hulm Solutions supports multi-location management. You can track inventory, sales, staff, and reporting separately per branch while viewing consolidated data across all locations from a single dashboard.
Does Hulm Solutions work on mobile devices?
Yes. Hulm Solutions includes a mobile POS interface. Sales staff can process transactions, check stock, and manage customer accounts directly from smartphones or tablets — ideal for businesses that need flexibility on the shop floor.
What is the difference between Hulm Solutions and a standard POS system?
A standard POS system only handles billing. Hulm Solutions is a full business operating system. It integrates POS with inventory, purchasing, vendor management, customer management, logistics, ecommerce, and analytics — all in one cloud platform. No third-party integrations needed.
Is Hulm Solutions available internationally?
Yes. While Hulm Solutions is built with Pakistani businesses as the primary focus — including local compliance and currency support — the underlying architecture is cloud-native and globally deployable. International businesses can use Hulm Solutions with region-specific configuration.
