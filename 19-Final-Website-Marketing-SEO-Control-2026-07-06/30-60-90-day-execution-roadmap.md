# 30-60-90 Day Execution Roadmap

## First 30 Days: Control The Base

### Technical SEO

- Fix `Nova Nordisk` to `Novo Nordisk`.
- Add 301 redirect from old typo URL to corrected URL.
- Verify Google Search Console.
- Submit sitemap.
- Confirm all 36 sitemap URLs are indexed or discoverable.
- Confirm GA4 events for contact form and CTA clicks.
- Confirm HubSpot tracking script loads correctly.
- Change protocol-relative HubSpot script to explicit `https://`.

### Content

- Expand `/contact` above 350 words.
- Expand `/ecosystem/hospitello` above 350 words.
- Add missing FAQ sections only where source-backed.
- Review all case study pages for brand spelling and client name accuracy.

### Marketing

- Create LinkedIn launch sequence for the completed website.
- Create 10 case-study social posts.
- Create 6 product spotlight posts.
- Create 3 partnership outreach email templates.

## Days 31-60: Build Authority

### SEO Content

Publish 8 support assets:

1. Product engineering company for industry SaaS platforms
2. What is a SaaS operating company?
3. Building software platforms for real industry operations
4. Retail operations platforms: HULM POS and Dunkin' Donuts proof
5. Healthcare software platforms: Hospitello, Novo Nordisk, and AGP proof

### Internal Linking

- Every article links to one product page.
- Every article links to one case study page.
- Every article links to `/contact`.
- Every product page links to relevant case studies.
- Every case study links back to `/case-studies` and `/contact`.

### Distribution

- Publish 2 LinkedIn posts per week.
- Run founder/CEO commentary posts around operating model and case study lessons.
- Begin direct outreach to system integrators and enterprise collaboration targets.

## Days 61-90: Convert And Optimize

### SEO Optimization

- Use GSC data to identify high-impression, low-CTR pages.
- Rewrite titles/metas for low-CTR pages.
- Add FAQs to pages receiving impressions from question-style queries.
- Refresh internal links based on landing page performance.

### CRO

- Review contact form completion rate.
- Add page-specific CTA copy if conversion is low.
- Add inquiry routing by product/industry.
- Build thank-you page or form success event.

### Marketing

- Package 3 case studies into PDF sales assets.
- Build one partnership landing-page variant if partnership traffic grows.
- Launch targeted LinkedIn campaign around case study proof if budget is approved.

## 90-Day Success Criteria

- Technical SEO remains clean.
- Corrected Novo Nordisk URL is live and indexed-ready.
- Sitemap has no wrong or typo URLs.
- Organic impressions baseline is established.
- First SEO/content performance review completed.
- Contact form conversions are tracked.
- At least 8 new SEO support assets are published.
- At least 20 LinkedIn/company distribution posts are published.
